About Albin

Albin is a brave and curious squirrel who embarks on a quest to restore peace in the squirrel kingdom. Along the way, he encounters various challenges and meets new friends.

Game Features

  • Collect acorns to earn points
  • Unlock special power-ups
  • Explore different squirrel habitats
  • Challenge your friends in multiplayer mode

System Requirements

To play Albin and the Squirrels, you need:

  • Operating System: Windows 95/98/ME
  • Processor: Pentium III 500 MHz
  • Memory: 128 MB RAM
  • Graphics: 16-bit color
  • Storage: 50 MB available space
